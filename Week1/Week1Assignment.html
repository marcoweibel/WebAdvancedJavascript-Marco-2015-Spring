With Darker Than Wax, the music has been reduced to its most concentrated form. Its pretty pure and simple, really. Founded by Funk Bast*rd and Kaye (Cosa Nostra), the label is basically a statement and a collection of like-minded musicans, artists and the like.

A statement on the pervasiveness of black music and the influence it has had on countless genres and sub genres. A statement that its all about bringing it into the future.

A statement that music should have depth, and that your racial/genetic/cultural make-up is irrelevant, Only your soul is.

We move.
