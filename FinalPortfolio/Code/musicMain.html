Marco Weibel (NY)

Darker Than Wax


Brought up on a soul diet, DJ / selector Marco, dabbles in things deep, funky, soulful and raw. One of the brightest selectors to have emerged out of the South-East Asian scene recently, his musical palette spans across a wide range of sounds from Jazz, Hip Hop, Boom Bap, Boogie, Afro/Brazilian, Deep/Jackin House, UK Bass and beyond.

This talented cat has shared dance-floors with established tastemakers such as Francois K, Scratcha DVA (Hyperdub, UK), Opolopo (Tokyo Dawn), Krystal Klear (All City), Pablo Valentino (FACES/MCDE) to name a few. Most recently, Marco played at major Singapore festival, Zoukout 2013 - Localised Tent. The festival is one of the biggest in the region. Be sure to expect a wide variety of head nodding frequencies to get down to while hes on the decks.

Marco is also a core member of music label/collective Darker Than Wax, which has been constantly pushing the boundaries of underground/leftfield music. Besides their mint catalogue of releases, which have been getting nods and plays from the likes of Rinse FM, Brownswood, Soulection, Majestic Casual and more, the collective is known for throwing some of the finest parties in town, confidently stamping their brand of music all over with nights that defy categorization, traverse the sound spectrum and leave you yearning for more.
