Marco Weibel

Design | Visuals | Interaction | Music Curation


Marco is a creative | designer | thinker | mover | shaker who is currently based in New York City. Originally from Singapore, he is pursuing his BFA at Parsons in Design & Technology. His interest lie in Graphic Design/Layouts, Generative/Algorhythmic Art, Video Art, Audio/Visual projects/installations, working with interactive spaces as well as most things music related.

Besides freelancing as a creative, Marco is also core member of music label Darker Than Wax, which has been pushing the boundaries of leftfield electronic music for the past 4 years. He manages the operations of the label in the US, handling event curation to merchandising. He also contributes to creative output of the label in the form of artworks and visuals. Having a deep passion for music, Marco also is one of the resident DJs for Darker Than Wax.

Feel free to reach out to Marco for projects / collaborations / coffee / beer.
